Maxio — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Everything stays on your device

Maxio stores all of your data — your logged exercises, scores, badges, and full workout history — locally on your device using on-device storage (Apple's SwiftData). Your data lives on your iPhone and nowhere else.

We never see your data

Maxio does not upload, sync, or transmit your workout data to our servers or to any third party. We do not operate a backend that collects, stores, or processes your personal fitness information. Because your data never leaves your device, we have no access to it.

No account, no sign-up

You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal identity to use Maxio. There are no usernames, no passwords, and no profiles tied to our systems.

Your control

You can delete any individual result, or clear your entire history at any time directly within the app. When you delete data, it is permanently removed from your device. Deleting the app removes all associated data stored on your device.

Purchases and payments

Maxio offers an optional one-time purchase called Maxio Pro. All purchase transactions, including payment, billing, and refunds, are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store and your Apple ID. Maxio never receives, stores, or has access to your payment details, billing address, credit card information, or any financial data. We do not maintain a backend server that tracks your purchase. The status of your purchase is verified locally on your device using Apple's StoreKit framework. Apple's privacy policy governs the payment transaction itself.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be made available within the app and on this page. Continued use of Maxio after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about privacy in Maxio, you can reach out through the app's App Store support page.

Frequently asked

Where is my data stored? All your data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server, and no account is required.

How does scoring work? Every rep counts as 0.1 points, and every 10 seconds of plank counts as 0.1 points. Earn Bronze (5+ pts), Silver (10+ pts), or Gold (20+ pts).

What does Maxio Pro include? Pro unlocks Plank, Pull-ups, and Dumbbell Bicep Curls, plus up to 3 custom exercises. It is a one-time purchase of $14.99 USD — no subscription.

Will I be charged again? No. Maxio Pro is a single one-time purchase. You pay once and keep access permanently on the Apple ID used for the purchase.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device? Open the app, go to the menu, and tap "Restore Purchases" while signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I request a refund? Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request one.
