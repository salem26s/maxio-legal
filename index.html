Maxio — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Everything stays on your device

Maxio stores all of your data — your logged exercises, scores, badges, and full workout history — locally on your device using on-device storage (Apple's SwiftData). Your data lives on your iPhone and nowhere else.

We never see your data

Maxio does not upload, sync, or transmit your workout data to our servers or to any third party. We do not operate a backend that collects, stores, or processes your personal fitness information. Because your data never leaves your device, we have no access to it.

No account, no sign-up

You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal identity to use Maxio. There are no usernames, no passwords, and no profiles tied to our systems.

Your control

You can delete any individual result, or clear your entire history at any time directly within the app. When you delete data, it is permanently removed from your device. Deleting the app removes all associated data stored on your device.

Subscriptions

If you purchase a subscription, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Maxio never receives or stores your payment details. Apple's privacy policy governs that transaction.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be made available within the app and on this page. Continued use of Maxio after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about privacy in Maxio, you can reach out through the app's App Store support page.
