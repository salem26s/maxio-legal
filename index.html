Privacy PolicyTerms of UseSupport Maxio — Privacy Policy Last updated: June 2026 Everything stays on your device Maxio stores all of your data — your logged exercises, scores, badges, and full workout history — locally on your device using on-device storage (Apple's SwiftData).
